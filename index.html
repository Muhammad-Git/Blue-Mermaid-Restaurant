About

Our Story

Blue Mermaid Restaurant IS A FUN, CASUAL DINING RESTAURANT LOCATED IN MALL OF THE EMIRATES AND DUBAI MALL.

The menu has been inspired by the amazing food and flavours of Africa, an extremely diverse continent, with numerous tribes, who not only speak different languages,

they also have very different foods, which they prepare and serve to their family and friends with a lot of pride.

Tribes offers a memorable fusion of exotic flavors, taken from the African continent since the Tribal era, influenced by the Spice Islands of the East, the French in the West, the Malay & Dutch in the South and the Arabic flavours of the North.
